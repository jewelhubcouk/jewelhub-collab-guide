📋 Step 3 of 5 — Your Requirements

What you need to do

Clear requirements. No surprises. Please read carefully before applying.

  • 1
    Receive your gifted order — unbox it, try it on, wear it for a few days before creating content.
  • 2
    Create two pieces of content minimum — for example a TikTok or Reel PLUS an Instagram post, or a blog post PLUS a social share. One piece is not enough.
  • 3
    Submit a Judge.me review — compulsory for all creators. Go to the product page on jewelhub.co.uk, scroll to reviews, and leave your honest review. This is not optional.
  • 4
    Tag @jewelhubcouk and use #JewelHubUK in every post.
  • 5
    Share your affiliate link in your bio or caption — your audience gets 10% off and you earn commission on every sale.
  • 6
    Disclose it's gifted — "gifted by @jewelhubcouk" — as required by UK ASA guidelines.
  • 7
    Submit your content links via our submission form once everything is live. This activates your commission tracking.
